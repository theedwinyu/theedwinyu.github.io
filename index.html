M.O.E.G.A. (Making Online Education Great Again)

Won 1st place at CatHacks VI!

M.O.E.G.A. is an education platform to simulate in-person lectures geared towards elementary & middle school students.

Teachers can create and invite their students to personal classrooms, where they can take lecture notes using a shared whiteboard and live-stream their audio by speaking into a microphone. When students physically raise their hands, they are granted a 30-second window to ask a question in the chat.
